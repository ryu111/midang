Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Overview

MiDang (迷檔) is an AI-powered historical mystery video channel. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you interact with our content and services.

Information We Collect

TikTok Integration

When you interact with our TikTok integration, we may access:

  • Your TikTok public profile information (username, display name)
  • Content posting permissions (to publish videos on your behalf, only with your explicit consent)

We do not collect or store:

  • Your TikTok password
  • Your private messages
  • Your browsing history
  • Your financial information

How We Use Your Information

  • To publish video content to your authorized social media accounts
  • To improve content quality based on aggregated performance data
  • To respond to your comments and messages

Data Storage and Security

  • We do not store personal data on our servers
  • API tokens are stored securely and encrypted
  • We use OAuth 2.0 for all platform authentication

Third-Party Services

We integrate with:

  • TikTok (Content Posting API)
  • YouTube (Data API v3)
  • FFmpeg (local video processing, no data transmitted)

Your Rights

  • Revoke API access at any time through your TikTok account settings
  • Request deletion of any data we hold
  • Contact us for any privacy concerns

Contact

For privacy inquiries, please contact us through our GitHub repository.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
